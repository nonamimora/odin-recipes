Description

Traditional Norwegian lamb soup.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces sliced lamb meat
  • 1 head cabbage, cored and slice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons whole black peppercorns
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Put ingredients into pot.
  2. Pour water in and cover with lid.
  3. Boil, simmer over low heat for 2 hours.
