Mustikkakeitto

Description

Finnish blueberry soup.

Ingredients

  • 3 pints blueberries
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp. cornstarch
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Bring blueberries and water to a boil
  2. Add sugar, cinnamon and zest
  3. Gently stir for 5 minutes
  4. Let it cool and serve
